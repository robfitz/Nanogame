This production is an artistic representation of the state of the art Laboratories and Cleanroom at the Nanoscience Centre, University of Cambridge. To keep the interface simple and playful some health and safety elements (fire exits, warning notices, etc...) and infrastructure (gas supply pipes) have been omitted, but otherwise - this is an authentic Nanotechnology research facility, where the future is made
